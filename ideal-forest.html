How to play

  • Enter your Player name to load points from the global leaderboard (other games).
  • Click/tap the canvas to move there.
  • Plant tree: costs 1000 pts.
  • Water a selected tree: costs 500 pts and enables growth.
  • Build windmill: costs 5000 pts; generates passive points over time.
  • Save stores your forest layout (trees/wind), points are shared globally.
